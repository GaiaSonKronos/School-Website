Transport Facilities

To and fro transportation for students, teachers and staff members is an important aspect of every school. Hence, DPS Howrah has appointed ‘Urban Services’ as the transportation service provider. Urban services will run buses covering Howrah and several important areas of Kolkata. The areas to be covered will be need based and decided in due course of time.

Urban Paribahan Services shall be offering transportation services independently and the school shall be in no way responsible or liable for any consequences arising out of it.
